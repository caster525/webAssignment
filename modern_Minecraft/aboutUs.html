Introduction to Modern Minecraft.

Modern Minecraft in a nutshell

Modern architecture in Minecraft has been popular since the dawn of its creation. We here at Modern Minecraft aim to enlighten more people to the world of modern designs, and guide you through designing more modern architecture.

Assisting you in design

We realize that designing in a modern way may be challenging for some. To assist you in your journey of building a modern abode, we have some guides on our website for you to reference.
